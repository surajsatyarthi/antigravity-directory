# Revenue Roadmap

## Goal
$1,000—$2,000 USD/month within 6 months.
At India cost of living, $1,000/month = financial independence.

## The Three Phases

---

## Phase 1 — Build (Now to Month 2)
Focus: Quality + Content + Indexing
Revenue target: $0—$300/month
Actions:
- Complete one-hour quality sprint (rules, prompts, MCP descriptions)
- Submit to Google Search Console, get indexed
- Launch on Product Hunt (one-time traffic spike)
- Write 2 foundation articles (MCP guide, Antigravity guide)
- Write 1 article per week
- Reach out to real sponsors (replace dummy ads)
- Target: get first paying sponsor at $300/month

---

## Phase 2 — Grow (Month 2 to Month 4)
Focus: SEO traction + First revenue
Revenue target: $300—$1,000/month
Milestones:
- 5,000+ monthly visitors
- 3 category sponsors × $300 = $900/month
- Newsletter at 500+ subscribers
Actions:
- Continue 1 article per week
- Add multi-IDE coverage (Cursor, Windsurf resources)
- Expand rules to 200+
- Build lead magnet PDF ("Top 50 MCP Servers for Antigravity")
- Pitch homepage banner sponsor ($800/month)

---

## Phase 3 — Monetise (Month 4 to Month 6)
Focus: Job board + Scale revenue
Revenue target: $1,000—$2,000/month
Milestones:
- 10,000—50,000 monthly visitors
- Job board launched
- Multiple sponsors
Revenue stack:
- 5 category sponsors × $300 = $1,500/month
- Job board 5 listings/month × $150 = $750/month
- Total: $2,250/month (target achieved)

Pricing to charge:
- Standard listing: $99 for 30 days
- Featured listing: $199 for 30 days (top of page)
- Bundle: 3 listings for $249

Target customers:
- Startups building with Google Antigravity
- Companies hiring AI developers
- Agencies looking for Antigravity-skilled freelancers

How to get first job postings:
- Email 20 AI startups directly — offer first listing free
- Post in Antigravity developer communities
- Add "Post a Job" button to site now (even before launch)

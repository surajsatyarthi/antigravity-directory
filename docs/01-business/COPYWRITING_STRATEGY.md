## POSITIONING

**We are the cursor.directory for Google Antigravity.**

The go-to place for every Antigravity developer to discover Skills, Rules, MCP Servers, Prompts, Agents, Workflows, Boilerplates, Tutorials, and Cheatsheets.

## TONE & VOICE

- Direct, developer-friendly, no hype
- Content-first — the resources are the product, not us
- Like cursor.directory: clean, minimal, lets the content speak
- No "earn money" messaging, no creator economy language
- No fake social proof, no invented testimonials

## RESOURCE CARD COPY

- Title: name of the resource
- Description: one line, what it does
- Tags: framework/language/tool tags in monospace
- Meta: star count (if GitHub), category tag
- Actions (on hover): Copy link, Bookmark

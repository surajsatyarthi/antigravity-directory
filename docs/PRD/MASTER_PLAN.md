## 5. Technical Architecture & Compliance

### 5.1. Bootstrapped Ingestion Strategy (The Growth Hack)

Recognizing our bootstrapped status, we utilize **Smart Scraping** as our primary Q1 fuel:

- **Stealth Protocol**: 1-5s randomized jitter, User-Agent rotation, and human-imitation via Playwright to mimic organic browsing.
- **Safety Thresholds**: Strictly adhering to platform "Soft-Limits" to mitigate risk of IP bans while maximizing data velocity.
- **Transition**: Move to Official APIs (GitHub GraphQL, X v2) ONLY once revenue milestones ($1k+ MRR) are met.

### 5.2. Compliance & Operations

- **Privacy**: GDPR/CCPA Ready (Easy opt-out/Data deletion).
- **Branding**: "Agentic Resource Hub" - Independent metadata layer.
- **Moderation**: Human-in-the-loop review for all community submissions.

  - **AM**: SEO/pSEO maintenance.
